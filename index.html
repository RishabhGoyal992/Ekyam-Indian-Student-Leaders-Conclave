About Us

The word EKYAM means "Oneness for a Righteous Purpose". Ekyam is being organised on the theme "Student Unions For Nation Building"

EKYAM is organized for the purpose of making the Student Unions across India to congregate at a single location and then harvest off the synergy created to work for the betterment of the students across India.

EKYAM intends to bring Student Unions to work further with the Government of India, its bodies and various other not for profit organizations on various development projects, thus making students contribute to nation building.

EKYAM is being organized between 14th-16th September on the sidelines of International Day of Democracy(15th September). We believe Student Unions are the best platforms to imbibe the values of democracy into the youth of the country and to encourage them to work for the nation. At Ekyam, elected representatives of Student Unions from all over the country will unite in Delhi to collaborate for Nation Building.

The events during the conclave will include a spectrum of activities which can benefit every attendee. These include general discussions, workshops, panel discussions and keynote speeches on various important issues. The event also will have sessions where bodies of the Government and various Non-Profits can pitch some of their problem statements for the colleges to solve.
